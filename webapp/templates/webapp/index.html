About Bushie Holdings

Bushie Holdings is registered as Close Corporation on the 15/10/2010 By Mr Bushie Tshepo Van Wyk and it started to be active after inception early 2011. Bushi Holdings has broad spectrum of partnerships is testament to our membership of select communities that are professionally certified and rigorously trained to complement and extend our partners’ global service capabilities.

WE DELIVER IN TIME AND ON BUDGET

Location
